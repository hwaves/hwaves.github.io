 About me

I am a PhD student in the System and Networking Group (SNG), led by Prof. Jiwu Shu and Prof. Qiao Xiang. My research interests center around intelligent models: (1) Application (Transformer-based reading comprehension). (2) Efficiency(efficient word embedding, sentence embedding, and training coreset). (3) Security (SMT-based unified verification and interpretation). (4) Related underlying software and system ecology(Python package dependency resolving, RDMA communication, and EB-level data center storage system). Along this direction, I have published more than a dozen papers. For more information, please find me in my CV.
